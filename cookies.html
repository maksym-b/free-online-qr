Cookie Policy

How cookies and similar technologies are used on this website.

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Overview

This website uses cookies only for analytics purposes and only if you choose to accept them. You can change your cookie preference at any time via Cookie settings in the footer.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They can be used to remember preferences, enable core functionality, or measure how a website is used.

How we use cookies

If you consent, we use cookies to measure aggregated website usage. These measurements help us understand which pages are visited and how the site performs, so we can improve the experience.

We do not use cookies for advertising, profiling, or selling data to third parties.

Analytics cookies

If you accept analytics cookies, Google Analytics may set cookies on your device. These cookies help us understand usage trends such as approximate location (country/region), device type, and pages visited.

If you do not consent, analytics scripts are not loaded and analytics cookies are not set.

Strictly necessary cookies

This website does not intentionally set strictly necessary cookies for normal operation. In some cases, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may set a short-lived security cookie when protecting the site against malicious traffic. These cookies are used for security and are not used for advertising.

Managing your preferences

You can manage cookie consent through the banner shown when you first visit the website. You can also change your choice at any time by opening Cookie settings.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete existing cookies, or configure site-level permissions. Note that blocking all cookies may affect analytics measurement but should not prevent QR code generation.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the Last updated date at the top of this page.
