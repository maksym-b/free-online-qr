QR applications

Below are real-life examples of how QR codes are used every day.

Websites

Send people to a page, menu, portfolio, store, or landing page in one scan.

Business cards

Share contact details instantly using a vCard QR code.

Wi-Fi access

Let guests connect without typing a password.

Payments

Link to payment pages or payment instructions for quick checkout.

Restaurant menus

Share a menu link without printing new menus every time.

Events and tickets

Speed up entry with QR tickets or share event details and schedules.

Posters and flyers

Turn print materials into interactive campaigns, sign-ups, or downloads.

Product packaging

Add instructions, warranty info, authenticity checks, or product pages.

WhatsApp chat

Start a conversation instantly with a prefilled phone number.

Phone calls

Make it easy to call support, reception, or a sales line.

Email links

Let users send you an email with a preset subject and message.

Maps and locations

Share an exact spot or a place name, useful for venues and meetups.
