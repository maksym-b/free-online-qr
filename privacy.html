Privacy Statement

How we handle data when you use this website.

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Overview

This website provides a free QR code generator that runs entirely in your browser. We aim to minimize data collection and to be transparent about how information is processed.

QR codes are generated client-side. The content you enter into the generator is not automatically transmitted to or stored by us.

Analytics are used only to understand general site usage and only after you give consent.

Who we are

We operate this website to provide tools for creating static QR codes. If you have questions about this Privacy Statement or how data is handled, please use the Contact page.

QR code data

Information entered into the QR generator, such as website URLs, text, Wi-Fi credentials, or vCard details, is processed locally in your browser to generate a QR code. We do not receive this information as part of normal operation.

Contact form

When you submit the contact form, the information you provide is used solely to respond to your inquiry. Contact messages are not used for marketing or profiling.

This Privacy Statement will be updated when backend processing is introduced, including details on transmission and retention.

Hosting and security

The website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process limited technical data, such as IP address and request metadata, to deliver content securely and to protect against malicious traffic.

Cookies and analytics

Cookies are used only for analytics purposes and only if you choose to accept them. Analytics help us understand aggregated usage, such as page views and device types.

You can find more information in the Cookie Policy and manage your preferences at any time via Cookie settings in the footer.

Legal basis

Analytics data is processed based on your consent. Technical data required to operate and secure the website is processed based on legitimate interests. Contact form data is processed to respond to your request.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data, and to withdraw consent for analytics at any time. Requests can be made via the Contact page.

Children

This website is not intended for children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the Last updated date at the top of this page.
